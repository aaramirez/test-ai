# How to Take a Quiz

## First Time Setup

1. Open opencode in the project directory
2. Type `/quiz-run` or `/quiz-practice`
3. The agent will ask for your participant ID (student number, employee ID, etc.)
4. Enter your ID when prompted
5. Enter your name when prompted
6. Enter your email (optional, needed for result delivery)
7. You are now registered — your info will be reused in future sessions

## Taking a Practice Quiz

1. Type `/quiz-practice`
2. Select the topic (bank) you want to practice
3. Select difficulty (easy, medium, hard) or leave blank for all
4. Select how many questions (or all)
5. Answer each question — you'll get immediate feedback:
   - ✅ Correct: see confirmation
   - ❌ Incorrect: see correct answer and explanation
6. At the end, review your score and explanations
7. Practice as many times as you want

## Taking a Live Quiz

1. Type `/quiz-run`
2. Confirm your identity (ID + name will be auto-filled from registry)
3. Select the topic (bank)
4. Select difficulty and count
5. Answer all questions — no feedback during the quiz
6. After submitting, you'll see a confirmation message — **no score is shown**
7. Results are saved and submitted to the admin for later evaluation
8. Your admin will send you your results once they've been evaluated

## Taking a Survey

1. Type `/quiz-run`
2. Confirm your identity
3. Select the survey bank
4. Answer all questions
5. Thank you — your responses have been recorded

## Tips

- Practice first, then go live when you're ready
- You can take the same quiz multiple times
- Your best score is tracked by the admin
- If you don't know your participant ID, ask your admin
