# How to Take a Survey

## What Are Surveys?

Surveys collect feedback and opinions. Unlike quizzes, there are no right or wrong answers — your responses help improve the learning experience.

## Taking a Survey

1. Open opencode in the project directory
2. Type `/survey`
3. The agent will ask for your participant ID
4. Enter your ID when prompted
5. The agent will list pending surveys available to you
6. Select the survey you want to take
7. Answer each question — select the option that best matches your opinion
8. After submitting, you'll see a confirmation message

## Visibility

Not all surveys are visible to everyone. Your group membership determines which surveys you can see:
- **Estudiantes**: Student-specific surveys
- **Instructores**: Instructor feedback surveys
- **All**: Surveys visible to everyone

If you don't see a survey you expected, contact your admin.

## Tips

- Be honest — your feedback matters
- You can only take each survey once
- Check back regularly for new surveys
- Your responses are anonymous unless you provide identifying information
